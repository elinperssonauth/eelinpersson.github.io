Elin Persson made her debut with the young adult novel The Afghan Sons (2020), winner of the 2021 Nordic Council Children and Young People’s Literature Prize. Something is Pulling at Me was published in 2021, followed by Summer in 2023.

Elin is currently working as editor-in-chief of the cultural magazine Provins and writes regularly in Arbetarbladet.

Her latest novel Pizzeria Roma, released in March 2025 to critical acclaim, was published by Wahlström & Widstrand.
